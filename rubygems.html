Using Bundler with Rubygem gemspecs

If you already have a gem with a gemspec, you can generate a Gemfile for your gem. 
$ bundle init --gemspec
If you are creating a new gem, simply create a new Gemfile.
Gems in the default group are regular dependencies, and gems in the :development group are development dependencies. Gems in other groups will not be included in the gemspec. 
source :rubyforge
gem "json"

group :development do
  gem "rspec"
end

group :debugging do
  gem "ruby-debug"
  gem "awsome_print"
end
Inside your gemspec, load bundler and replace all of the dependency calls with a single call to #add_bundler_dependencies. 
require 'bundler'
Gem::Specification.new do |s|
  # [...]
  s.add_bundler_dependencies
end
That's it! You can now build your gem. 
$ gem build my_gem.gemspec
